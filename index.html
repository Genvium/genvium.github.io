Funding Made Simple for Startups

Genvium is a desktop platform designed to simplify how startups find and secure funding. We aggregate funding opportunities into a centralized, searchable database with powerful filters, making it easy to discover grants, competitions, and investment options tailored to your needs.

Beyond discovery, Genvium provides direct links to external applications and tools to help you track your submissions, deadlines, and progress—all in one place. Whether you're just starting out or scaling, Genvium streamlines the entire funding process so you can focus on building your business.
